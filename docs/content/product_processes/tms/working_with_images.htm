Working with Images

Summary:

Describes the process to add images in the TRSS project.

Solution:

Inserting Images

The TRSS project uses thumbnails for most images. Additionally, add Screen Tips and Alternate Text when importing images.

  1. Create a new paragraph for the image.
  2. Click the Insert tab, and then click Image.
  3. Navigate to the image that you want to insert.
  4. On the General tab of the Insert Image window:
  5. Click Style Class, and the select 25PercentThumb.
  6. For Screen Tip, enter the navigational information for TRSS page represented–for example, People > Roles > HR Manager.
  7. For Alternate Text, copy and paste the Screen Tip you just entered.
  8. Click OK.
