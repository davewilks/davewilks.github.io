Closing a Ticket and Sprint

Summary:

Describes the step to close a JIRA TCOMM ticket and a TCOMM sprint.

Full Description:

To close a ticket, you need to get it into the Development Complete status. There might be other statuses you can close it from such as Development Blocked.

To close a sprint, you need JIRA administrative rights. Contact the TComm Tech Lead (Dave Wilks) for permissions.

Solution:

  1. Make sure the ticket status is Development Complete or a status where the Workflow button is available. It can be assigned or unassigned.
  2. Click Workflow, and then Recommend Close.

  3. On the Recommend Close transition screen, select one of the following Resolution values:
    • Fixed/Complete - To indicate it's already documented or fixed in some way.
    • Will Not Pursue - To indicate that it doesn't need to be documented.
    • Duplicate - To indicate it's a, um, duplicate.

  4. Click Confirm Closed.

  5. Once all the tickets are closed in the sprint or moved to other sprints, in the board click Active Sprints.
  6. Select the drop-down arrow next to the sprint name.
  7. Click Complete Sprint. Once you close/complete a sprint, you cannot reopen it. (The version of JIRA we use does not allow this functionality.) You would need to recreate a new sprint (with the same name).
  8. To view the closed sprint, in the board click Reports.
  9. Click Sprint Report and select the sprint from the drop-down list.
