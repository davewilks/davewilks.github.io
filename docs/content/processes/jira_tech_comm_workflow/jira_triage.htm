JIRA Triage - OBSOLETE

Summary:

This process is superseded by the sprint planning process.

Solution:

The triage process involves scheduling tickets for sprints.

  1. Identify the sprint that the ticket will be documented in.
  2. Open the appropriate Agile board:
    • TRSS: http://jira.ehr.com/secure/RapidBoard.jspa?rapidView=111&view=planning
    • Wasabi: http://jira.ehr.com/secure/RapidBoard.jspa?rapidView=109&view=planning&selectedIssue=TCOMM-139
  3. Enter the estimates for each ticket without estimates.
  4. Drag the tickets into the appropriate sprint.
