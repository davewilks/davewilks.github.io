Pronouns

Summary:

Defines the use of pronouns based on Chicago Manual of Style.

Full Description:

According to CMS, use demonstrative pronouns when the antecedent is clear. Feel free to use "here." <this seems incomplete>

Demonstrative Pronouns Subject Pronouns* Object Pronouns* Relative Pronouns**
(To whom are you referring?)
this I me who
that you us whom
these we you which
those he him that
none she her  
neither it it  
  they them  

* For most documentation Tech Comm uses "you" and, when appropriate, "them" or "they". All other pronouns are generally not used.

** Which introduces non-restrictive clauses. That introduces restrictive clauses.
