Images and Animated GIFs

Summary:

Describes the use of multimedia in tutorials.

Full Description:

You can insert images and animated GIFS into Iridize guides. Images typically need to be sized small enough to fit within inline tip text. Animated GIFs add a little "sizzle" and are useful mini demos in getting started or introductory tutorials.
